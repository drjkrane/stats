Interactive Tool — Survival Comparison Calculator

Enter event/censoring counts for two arms to estimate a hazard ratio and log-rank significance, or enter median survival times to compare arms directly. Convention: Arm A is treated as the reference (e.g. control); the reported HR is for Arm B relative to Arm A — an HR below 1 means Arm B has a lower hazard (better survival) than Arm A.
